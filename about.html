ABOUT US

BLUDOT Service PLC is independent firm of consulting architects & engineers with an expertise in the planning, development, design, construction supervision and contract administration of a wide range of architectural & civil engineering projects in both the private and the public sectors. Our company has been in establishment since November, 2015, and since then we were able to obtain a Grade-I Consulting Architects & Engineers License Issued by the Construction Bureau of Addis Ababa City Administration. Our consulting firm is established as a private limited company in October 2009 incorporated according to Ethiopian Commercial Code and The Company is owned by Ethiopian shareholders who have extensive experience in the construction sector; Eng. Michael Yilma & Architect Mikias Eshetu who are the co-founders & co-shareholders of the PLC.
